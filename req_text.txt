School CRM Development Requirement Document
 – Cocoonz Preschool & School
Project Overview
Develop a School CRM and Fee Management System to manage student admissions, branches,
classes, fee collection, receipts, reports, and due tracking.
Modules
1. Branch Management 2. Student Management 3. Class Management 4. Fee Structure
Management 5. Fee Collection 6. Receipt Generation 7. Due Management 8. Reports 9.
Dashboard 10. User Roles
Fee Heads
Admission Fee, Tuition Fee, Book Fee, Skill Development Charges, After School Activities Fee,
Daycare Fee
Fee Types
Full Fee: 5% discount on Tuition Fee. Term Wise: Term 1, Term 2, Term 3.
Student Fields
Admission Number, Student Name, Class, Branch, Fee Type, Parent Details, Contact Information.
Reports
Daily Collection, Monthly Collection, Branch-wise Collection, Outstanding Fees, Discount Report,
Student Lists.
User Roles
Super Admin, Branch Admin, Accountant, Teacher.
