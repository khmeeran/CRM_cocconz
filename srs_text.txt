Software Requirement Specification (SRS)
 Cocoonz Preschool & School CRM
1. Introduction
This document defines requirements for a multi-branch School CRM and Fee Management System.
2. Objectives
Manage admissions, fee collection, branches, classes, daycare, activities, reporting and
communication.
3. User Roles
Super Admin, Branch Admin, Accountant, Teacher, Parent (optional portal).
4. Branch Management
Create and manage multiple school branches with separate fee structures and reports.
5. Student Management
Admission, profile management, class allocation, parent details and document management.
6. Fee Structure Module
Admission Fee, Tuition Fee, Book Fee, Skill Development Charges, After School Activities Fee and
Daycare Fee.
7. Fee Type Logic
Full Fee with 5% tuition discount and Term Wise collection with Term 1, Term 2 and Term 3.
8. Admission Workflow
Enquiry → Registration → Admission → Fee Assignment → Receipt Generation.
9. Fee Collection Workflow
Generate dues, collect payments, print receipts, track balances and overdue amounts.
10. Dashboard
Collection summary, dues, admissions, branch performance and monthly trends.
11. Reports
Daily, Monthly, Branch-wise, Class-wise, Outstanding, Discount and Collection reports.
12. Notifications
SMS, WhatsApp and Email reminders for due fees and announcements.
13. Database Tables

Branches, Classes, Students, Parents, Fee Structure, Student Fee Mapping, Collections, Receipts,
Users and Roles.
14. Security
Role-based access, audit logs, backups and secure authentication.
15. Technical Stack
Frontend: React. Backend: Laravel/ASP.NET Core. Database: MySQL/PostgreSQL. Mobile App:
Flutter.
16. Future Enhancements
Attendance, Transport, Payroll, Inventory, Parent App and Learning Management.
